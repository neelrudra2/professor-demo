Projects and Research

Core domains, ongoing projects, and research contributions

Dr. Kamalesh Debnath's research focuses on advanced techniques in Machine Learning, Signal Processing, and Wireless Communication, with applications in intelligent communication systems and embedded platforms.

Core Research Areas

Machine Learning

Deep learning for signal enhancement, pattern recognition, feature extraction, and intelligent communication systems.

Signal Processing

Adaptive filters, OFDM systems, noise reduction algorithms, FPGA-based DSP systems, and real-time processing.

Wireless Communication

5G/6G systems, MIMO, modulation techniques, fading channel analysis, and spectrum optimization.

Current Projects

Machine Learning-Based Noise Cancellation for Wireless Signals

2023 – Ongoing

Developing adaptive ML models that enhance signal clarity in multipath and noisy environments. Includes real-time implementation on embedded hardware.

FPGA-Based DSP System for Real-Time Filtering

2022 – Ongoing

Designing low-power, high-speed filters on FPGA platforms for next-generation communication devices.
